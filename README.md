# Blueberry Escape
## Game Description: 
You play as a sentient blueberry looking to escape the farm you are trapped in. You must find keys in the grass to unlock the locked gates and progress through the farm. 
## Game Controls
You move with WASD and to pick up a key just collide with it and your key tracker will go up. Every time you use a key to open a gate the key tracker will go down to indicate you used the key.
## Developer Thoughts
I loosely followed the Week 4 tutorial in order to figure out the kind of game I should make. I used a top down camera view like the tutorial and thought about what kind of collectables there can be in my game. I honestly designed the player character before I knew an idea for the story of the game and just built off of that. I used aseprite to make the sprites for my game and I lot of time trying to fix the fence design but the final project still looks strange but I think it's fine. I originally just wanted a simple game but I kind of last track of time and just started building more. This program really helps me get into the groove of just making things and being creative that I just wouldn't have if I tried making a game completely solo. I really like godot and every time a I make a new game I can see the small improvements on mistakes I have made in the past and just getting better at using godot. I thoroughly enjoyed making this game and I hope you like it too even if it is pretty short.
